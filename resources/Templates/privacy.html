Privacy for Budgetcontrol Application

Effective Date: 2024 September 1

1. General Principles
The Budgetcontrol application is a free, Open Source project developed by MlabFactory and managed by Marco De Felice, with its registered office in Somma Lombardo (VA), Italy. We are committed to processing personal data transparently and securely, in accordance with European privacy regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This policy provides information on what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data Collected
While using the Budgetcontrol application, we may collect the following personal data: Identification data: email address for account creation and other voluntarily provided information such as name, surname, date of birth. Technical data: information about how you use the app, including log data and usage details through analytics tools. Transactional data: if you use the app's features to monitor your finances, we will collect details about income, expenses, categories, and other related information.

3. Purpose of Data Processing
The collected data will be used to: Provide the services offered by the app, including technical support and system notifications. Improve the application and analyze its usage to optimize the user experience. Send relevant communications related to the services offered, including updates and promotions, where you have provided consent.

4. Data Retention
We will retain your personal data as long as you actively use the application. If you do not access your account for more than 24 months, your data will be deactivated and subsequently deleted. You can also request the deletion of your data at any time.

5. Data Sharing
Your data will not be shared with third parties unless it is necessary to fulfill legal obligations, or you have given explicit consent. For example, we may share your data with technical service providers (e.g., hosting) or legal advisors to protect our rights.

6. Data Security
We adopt appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. All data is processed in compliance with ISO 27001 security standards and undergoes regular checks.

7. Your Rights
You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data, as well as to object to its processing or request its restriction. You can exercise your rights by contacting us at info@budgetcontrol.cloud.
